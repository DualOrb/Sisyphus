# Scenario: Courier Delayed at Customer Location

**Source:** Dispatch Analyst Guide -- Scenarios section, Courier Support (Customer Not Responding)

---

## When This Happens

Couriers are occasionally delayed attempting to find a customer or confirm delivery with the customer. This can happen when there is no unit number, the customer does not answer the door, the address is hard to locate, or the customer is unresponsive.

---

## Procedure

### Step 1: Communicate with and Help the Courier Find the Customer

- Message or call the courier to understand the situation. Are they at the right address? Is it an apartment with no unit number? Is the customer not answering?
- If there is no unit number on the delivery address:
  1. Click on the customer's email address in the order.
  2. Check past orders for a unit number.
  3. Message the customer from the current order asking them to meet the courier at the front door or main entrance.

### Step 2: Attempt to Reach the Customer

- Message the customer: "Courier is at your location with your order."
- If the customer does not respond to the message, call them directly.
- Use the 4 contact circles on the dispatch page for quick access to customer contact options (available above the customer's address on the dispatch view, or above their full name in the order page).

### Step 3: Instruct Courier to Leave Order at Door

If the customer remains unresponsive after messaging and calling:

1. Instruct the courier to take or send a photo of the order left at the door, including the address or door number in the photo.
2. Have the courier leave the order at the door.
3. The courier should mark the order as delivered.

### Step 4: Notify the Customer of the Delivery with the Photo

- Send the customer a notification that their order has been delivered and left at the door.
- Include or reference the photo taken by the courier showing the delivery location.
- If/when the customer contacts Valley Eats later, explain the situation: the courier attempted to reach them, waited, and left the order at the door with photo confirmation.

---

## Impact on Other Orders

- While the courier is stuck at a customer location, their other assigned orders may be delayed.
- Check the courier's upcoming pickups and deliveries.
- If the delay is extending beyond a few minutes, consider rerouting the courier's next orders to another available courier to prevent cascading delays.
- Use the courier utilization heads-up display to identify available couriers (Blue = empty, Green = OK).

---

## Delivery Instructions

- Check the "Delivery Instructions" section on the order for any notes the customer may have left.
- If the address has known issues (hard to find, special access), these may be noted in the customer's "Background" section.
- After resolving the issue, update delivery instructions on the customer's account so this information is available for future orders.

---

## Key Principle

Do not let the courier wait indefinitely. After a reasonable attempt to contact the customer (message + call), proceed with leaving the order at the door with photo documentation. The courier needs to continue their route. Every minute spent waiting compounds delays on their remaining orders.
