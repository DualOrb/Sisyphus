# Courier Pay Adjustment -- Policies and Procedures

> Source: Dispatch Analyst Guide -- Adjusting courier pay on an order, Courier Earnings

## Courier Base Pay Structure

- **$4.50** for the first 5 km (restaurant-to-customer distance, not courier's current location).
- **$0.61/km** after the first 5 km (base range).
- **$0.80/km** after the first 5 km if the customer is in the extended delivery range.
- **100% of the customer tip** goes to the courier.
- Payouts are issued at the end of each week for orders delivered the previous week.

## What Is a Top-Up?

When drivers deliver orders a certain distance from one town to another, they may be eligible for a **top-up** -- an additional payment added to the order. Top-ups compensate for the extra time and fuel required for cross-market deliveries.

## How to Add a Top-Up

1. Open the order by clicking on the order ID.
2. Click **Delivery Adjustments** at the top of the order.
3. Enter the top-up amount.

**CRITICAL: Top-ups should not be added to an order until it is completed/delivered.**

## Top-Up Policies by Market

### Carleton Place to Almonte

**Eligible**: A Carleton Place driver gets an Almonte-to-Almonte order (pick up in Almonte, deliver to Almonte address).

- Top-up: **$4.00 + $0.50 gas = $4.50 total**

**Not eligible**:
- If the driver gets 3 or more consecutive Almonte orders while already in Almonte, they no longer qualify for the top-up.
- If the order goes from Carleton Place to Almonte (not Almonte-to-Almonte).
- If the driver picks up in Almonte and delivers back to Carleton Place.

### Embrun to Casselman

**Eligible**: An Embrun driver gets a Casselman-to-Casselman order (pick up in Casselman, deliver to Casselman address).

- Top-up: **$4.00 + $0.50 gas = $4.50 total**

**Not eligible**:
- If the driver gets 3 or more consecutive Casselman orders while already in Casselman, they no longer qualify for the top-up.
- If the order goes from Embrun to Casselman (not Casselman-to-Casselman).
- If the driver picks up in Casselman and delivers back to Embrun.

### Law & Orders Innisville

**Eligible**: A Carleton Place or Perth driver picks up from Law & Orders Innisville and delivers to a **Perth address**.

- Top-up: **$4.00 + $0.50 gas = $4.50 total**

**Not eligible**:
- If the courier picks up from Law & Orders Innisville and delivers to Carleton Place.

## Key Rules

1. **Always include the $0.50 gas top-up** alongside the $4.00 base top-up (total $4.50).
2. **Do not add the top-up until the order is completed/delivered.**
3. The "3 or more" rule: if a courier accumulates 3+ orders in the remote town while already there, they lose top-up eligibility because they are effectively working in that market, not making a special trip.
4. The top-up is specifically for when the courier must travel to a remote town to do a local delivery within that town -- not for standard cross-town deliveries.

## Courier Pay Questions

If a courier questions their delivery fee:
- Explain the pay structure: $4.50 base for first 5 km, $0.61/km after, 100% of tip.
- If they believe there is a discrepancy, ask them to email relations@valleyeats.ca with the order number.
- If a courier refuses an order because the pay is insufficient, try to persuade them. If they still refuse, move the order to another courier and create a ticket for driver relations.
