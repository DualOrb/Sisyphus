# Courier Earnings Context

**Trigger:** Courier asks about pay, delivery fees, tips, or payouts.

**Check First:**
- What specific pay concern does the courier have?

**Steps:**
1. **Base pay:** $4.50 for first 5 km (restaurant-to-customer distance), $0.61/km after (base range), $0.80/km after (extended range). System calculates automatically.
2. **Tips:** 100% of delivery tip goes to courier. Takeout tips go to restaurant. Dispatch does NOT see or consider tips when assigning orders.
3. **Payouts:** weekly (Sat-Fri) paid following Friday via direct deposit. Questions = relations@valleyeats.ca.
4. **Top-ups ($4.00 + $0.50 gas = $4.50):** Carleton Place driver doing Almonte-to-Almonte order, or Embrun driver doing Casselman-to-Casselman order, or CP/Perth driver doing Law & Orders Innisville to Perth. Not eligible if 3+ consecutive orders in remote town, or if going between towns (not within the remote town). Top-ups added only after order is completed.
5. **Courier doesn't understand fee:** explain the structure. **Thinks there was a mistake:** direct to relations@valleyeats.ca with order number. **Refuses order for low pay:** ask politely; if they refuse, move order to another courier and ticket relations@valleyeats.ca. **Only getting no-tip orders:** acknowledge frustration, explain dispatch doesn't see/consider tips.
6. **Courier points:** earned for availability submissions, picking up shifts, successful deliveries. Deducted for misdeliveries, dropped shifts. Redeemable in driver shop.

**Escalate If:**
- Courier believes there is a pay discrepancy (direct to relations@valleyeats.ca)
- Courier refuses multiple orders (ticket for relations@valleyeats.ca)
