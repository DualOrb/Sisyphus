# Perks Plus and Loyalty Program

**Trigger:** Customer asks about Perks Points, Perks Plus subscription, or service fee credits.

**Check First:**
- Customer's current Perks Points balance and Convenience Free Orders count on their profile

**Steps:**
1. **Perks Points:** earned automatically from qualifying orders. Visible on profile. Do NOT modify without supervisor instruction.
2. **Perks Plus ($11.95 + HST/month):** 3x Perks Points, free delivery within 5km, savings from participating restaurants, rewards/badges, surprise rewards.
3. **Service fee credits ("Convenience Free Orders"):** waive service fee at checkout, auto-applied. Add via "No fees" button on customer profile. Negative number to remove. Go-to resolution for small complaints.
4. **Never use the general "credit" button** on profile. Always credit through a specific order so notes are attached.
5. **Goodwill credits** = always issued as credits (not cash), always confirmed with supervisor first.

**Escalate If:**
- Customer disputes their Perks Points balance
- Perks Plus subscription billing issue
