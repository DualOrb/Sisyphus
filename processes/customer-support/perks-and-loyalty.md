# Perks Plus and Loyalty Program

> Source: Dispatch Analyst Guide -- Perks Plus section, Customers section

## Perks Points (Standard)

- Every customer earns Perks Points from qualifying orders placed through Valley Eats.
- Points accumulate automatically with each qualifying order.
- The customer's current point balance is visible on their profile under **"Perks Points"**.
- **Do not modify a customer's Perks Points without a supervisor's instruction.** There is no reason to touch these stats independently.

## Perks Plus (Paid Subscription)

Perks Plus is a paid subscription program. Customers sign up voluntarily.

### What Perks Plus Includes

- **3x Perks Points** on every order (triple the standard earning rate).
- **Free delivery** for orders within 5km of the restaurant.
- Savings on delivery from participating restaurants who are part of the program.
- Ability to **collect rewards** and receive **exclusive badges**.
- **Surprise rewards** that can be applied to eligible orders.

### Cost

- **$11.95 + HST per month**.

## Service Fee Credits ("Convenience Free Orders")

Service fee credits are separate from Perks Points. They waive the service fee on a customer's bill.

### How They Work

- If a customer has service fee credits, they are **automatically applied at checkout**.
- They are the go-to resolution for small customer complaints (minor issues, slight delays).

### How to Add Service Fee Credits

1. Open the customer's profile.
2. Click the **"No fees"** button in the top right corner.
3. Enter the number of credits to add.
4. To deduct credits, enter a **negative number**.

### What to Tell Customers

- Service fee credits are applied automatically at their next checkout.
- They waive the service fee on their bill.
- Credits do not expire (unless told otherwise by a supervisor).

## Key Rules

- Never modify Perks Points without supervisor instruction.
- Never use the general "credit" button on the customer profile. Always credit through a specific order so notes can be attached.
- Goodwill credits (extra compensation for inconvenience) must always be issued as credits, not cash refunds. Always confirm with a supervisor or manager before issuing goodwill credits.
