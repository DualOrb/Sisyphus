# Credits and Refunds -- How to Issue

**Trigger:** Need to issue a credit or refund after determining the remedy (see refund-policy.md for when).

**Check First:**
- Order details and the specific issue
- Whether this is a refund (back to payment method) or credit (store credit)

**Steps:**
1. Open the order > click "Order Corrections" (top right) > set resolution to "Refund."
2. **Choose fault:** "Restaurant Owes Valley Eats" = restaurant pays (their mistake). "Valley Eats Owes Restaurant" = we pay restaurant for remake (courier/VE error).
3. **Choose type:** "Refund Cash" = back to payment method (1-3 business days). "Refund Credits" = instant store credit (use if customer reordering right away).
4. Write detailed notes explaining what was done and why. Select driver error type if applicable. Save.
5. **Service fee credits (separate):** open customer profile > "No fees" button (top right) > enter number of credits. Negative number to remove.
6. **Never use the general "credit" button** on customer profile. Always go through a specific order so notes are attached.
7. **Goodwill credits** (extra compensation beyond issue value) must always be issued as credits (not cash refunds) and require supervisor approval.
8. **Sending proof:** customer profile > transaction history > click paper scroll icon > take screen snip (Shift+Win+S). Never copy the receipt link.

**Escalate If:**
- Major credits requiring supervisor approval
- Unclear fault assignment
