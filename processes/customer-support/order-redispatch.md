# Order Re-Dispatch

> Source: Dispatch Analyst Guide -- Order Re-Dispatch section, Wrong Address, Missing Items, Wrong Order

## What Is a Re-Dispatch

A re-dispatch sends food back out from the restaurant to be redelivered to the customer. It is used when an order was already delivered but had a problem (missing items, wrong order, wrong address, etc.).

## Pre-Dispatch Checklist

Before re-dispatching, confirm ALL of the following:

1. **The customer wants the food sent back out.** There is no point in re-dispatching if they are willing to settle for a credit.
2. **The customer is available** to receive the food once it is redelivered.
3. **The restaurant is still open** and they make the food.
4. **Enough drivers are available.** If there will be a delay, inform the customer of the delay before proceeding.
5. **The order has been marked as "Completed"** by the driver or dispatch.
6. **The order is from today's date.** Re-dispatching orders from previous days causes system issues.

## Before Re-Dispatching for Wrong/Missing Items

1. Confirm the customer has the correct order number on their bag.
   - If no number is written, check the restaurant's recent orders to rule out a misdelivery.
2. Ask the customer to send a picture of the receipt. This confirms whether the restaurant punched it in wrong. Also ask for pictures of any incorrect items as evidence.
3. **Confirm with the restaurant** that they are willing to accept the charge (for restaurant-fault re-dispatches, the restaurant pays the delivery fee).
4. Confirm the customer wants to receive the new order before proceeding.

## Step-by-Step Re-Dispatch Procedure

### Step 1: Open Order Corrections

- Open the order by clicking the order ID or entering it.
- Click **"Order Corrections"** in the top right of the page.

### Step 2: Set Resolution to Re-Dispatch

- In the Order Corrections screen, set the resolution to **"Re-Dispatch"**.

### Step 3: Choose Who Is at Fault

- Select whether it was a **courier mistake**, **restaurant mistake**, or **Valley Eats mistake**.

### Step 4: Write the Description

- In the description box, write what is being re-dispatched so the restaurant knows what to prepare.
- Example: "Please prepare the missing medium fries, thank you!"

### Step 5: Enter Remake Cost (if Valley Eats/Courier fault)

- If the issue is a Valley Eats or courier mistake, enter the cost of the item(s) being re-dispatched. This pays the restaurant for the remake.

### Step 6: Set Pickup Time

- If the market is not busy, set the pickup time for **10 minutes**.
- If the market is busy, set a later time to give couriers time to arrive at the restaurant.

### Step 7: Select Cause and Error Type

- Select the **cause** of the re-dispatch (Courier, Valley Eats, or Restaurant).
- Select the **error type** (Missing items, Incorrect order, Misdelivered, etc.).
- The driver who originally delivered the order is **automatically assigned** at the bottom. If a different driver was at fault, use the dropdown to select the correct courier's name.

### Step 8: Save and Verify

- Save the re-dispatch.
- Confirm the order appears on the dispatch screen.
- Ensure the order is assigned to a driver who is currently on shift (the original driver may have ended their shift).
- Notify the dispatcher of that market. Set the re-dispatch to the current market delay or ask the dispatcher when it works best.

## After Re-Dispatch

- The re-dispatched order is initially assigned to the **original driver** due to their familiarity with the customer's location. It may be rerouted for efficiency if the driver is unable to complete it.
- A **recycling symbol** will appear next to re-dispatched orders on the dispatch screen.
- **Follow up with the customer** after the re-dispatch is delivered to ensure everything is resolved. This is a best practice that makes customers feel valued.

## Canceling a Re-Dispatch

If you need to cancel a re-dispatch after it has been created:

1. Click the **green X** on the re-dispatch.
2. Set the order status to **"Completed"**.
   - This ensures the 1st courier (original delivery) gets paid.
   - The 2nd courier (assigned to re-dispatch) will still receive the delivery fee but not the tip.
3. If the restaurant needs to be paid for a remake, process it using Order Corrections with **"Valley Eats Owes Restaurant"**.

## Fault-Specific Procedures

### Restaurant at Fault (e.g., missing items, wrong food prepared)

- Re-dispatch with **Restaurant** selected as at fault.
- The restaurant pays the delivery fee.
- Ensure the restaurant knows when the driver will arrive.

### Courier at Fault (e.g., misdelivery, wrong bag given)

- Re-dispatch with **Courier/Driver Error** selected.
- Enter the subtotal of the items for the remake cost (Valley Eats pays the restaurant).
- Select "Driver Error" at the bottom of the re-dispatch menu.
- For wrong address re-dispatches: request a **different driver**, have them call the customer upon arriving, and take a photo including the house number.

### Valley Eats at Fault

- Re-dispatch with **Valley Eats** selected.
- Enter the cost of the items being re-dispatched (Valley Eats pays the restaurant for the remake).
