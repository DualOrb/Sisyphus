# Ticket Assignment and Reassignment

**Trigger:** New issue needs assignment, or a ticket needs to be passed to someone else.

**Check First:**
- Can you handle this yourself? Only reassign if you truly cannot.
- Who is the correct person/team for this type of issue?

**Steps:**
1. **Assign to yourself** immediately when a new issue comes in (click "assign to me").
2. **Before reassigning:** verify right person, include specific info on why you are passing it and what outcome is needed, reset status to "New."
3. **Routing:** Drop shift (Mon-Fri 8-4) = Driver Relations. Drop shift (evenings/weekends) = you (find replacement). Restaurant menu/portal/tablet = Supervisor > growth@valleyeats.ca. App/web tech issues = try troubleshooting first, then Supervisor > dev team. Repeated courier behavior = relations@valleyeats.ca. Customer wants supervisor = create ticket with all info. Fraud/stolen card = Supervisor immediately. Restaurant closed = next support agent when restaurant opens.
4. Never leave a ticket unassigned. Always mark reassigned tickets as "New."
5. Staff phone numbers are confidential -- never share with customers or restaurants without permission.

**Escalate If:**
- Possible fraud or stolen card (supervisor immediately)
- Customer requests to speak with supervisor
- You cannot resolve the issue after attempting all relevant steps
