# Vendor Portal Guide

**Trigger:** Need to access the Vendor Portal for menu, hours, or restaurant management tasks.

**Check First:**
- Access https://vendors.valleyeats.ca/ with dispatch credentials

**Steps:**
1. **Navigation:** restaurants listed alphabetically by market. Search via "Search Restaurant" tab (upper right). Click restaurant > Profile & Settings. Click "Menu" on left side for menu items.
2. **Disable menu item (Method 1):** Menu > browse to section > click pause button under item. **(Method 2):** use search bar > toggle "Available" off > click Publish.
3. **Disable toppings/sides/drinks in combos:** click "Modifiers" at top > search item > click into EACH section that appears > pause in every section. Multiple sections may exist.
4. **One-time hours change:** Hours > Holiday Hours > set date and adjusted times (auto-reverts).
5. **Permanent hours change:** Hours > select new time per day > Update Hours > refresh page to verify. Note change for Restaurant Relations Coordinator.
6. **Split hours: DO NOT TOUCH.** Ticket for technical team. Halt if needed.
7. **Halt restaurant:** change status to "Halted." Resets next morning automatically.
8. **Test tablet connectivity:** send test message from restaurant portal (tell restaurant not to touch tablet).
9. **What dispatch can do:** pause/re-enable items, adjust hours, disable modifiers, halt restaurants, send test messages, access menu portal. **NEVER delete items or photos.**

**Escalate If:**
- Split hours need editing (technical team only)
- Major menu restructuring needed (growth@valleyeats.ca)
